Ambulance Services

When every second counts, our ambulance services ensure swift response to emergencies, providing critical care en route to the nearest medical facility.

Alpha Ambulance

Dial 0711475475 for immediate assistance. Alpha Ambulance operates 24/7 across major cities and rural areas, equipped with advanced life-support systems.

RedCross Emergency Medical Services

Contact the Kenyan Red Cross Society at 1199 for emergency medical transportation. Their fleet of ambulances is stationed nationwide to respond promptly to emergencies.

National Hospital Ambulance Services

Leading hospitals like Aga Khan Hospital (Call: +254 20 3662000) and Nairobi Hospital (Call: +254 20 2845000) provide ambulance services for inter-hospital transfers and emergency response.

MediCross Ambulance Services

Call 0722-123-456 for emergency medical assistance. MediCross operates a network of ambulances equipped with advanced medical equipment and staffed by experienced paramedics.

St. John Ambulance

Contact St. John Ambulance at 0721-123-456 for emergency medical services. They provide pre-hospital care, first aid training, and ambulance services across Kenya.

MedicEast Ambulance

For emergency medical assistance, call MedicEast Ambulance at 0733-123-456. They offer rapid response and transportation to medical facilities with trained paramedics onboard.

Paramedics Plus

Paramedics Plus operates a fleet of ambulances equipped with advanced medical equipment. Call 0710-123-456 for emergency medical assistance and transportation to hospitals.

LifeCare Ambulance Services

LifeCare Ambulance Services can be reached at 0708-123-456. They provide emergency medical response and transportation to medical facilities with trained medical professionals.
